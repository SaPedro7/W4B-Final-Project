Rainbow Popsicle



Along with ice cream, colorful and flavorful popsicles (or ice pops) are the perfect choice to cool you down from the heat of summer. They are a piece of water or milk-based ice flavoured with fruit and served on a stick. Unlike ice cream or sorbet, which are whipped while freezing to prevent ice crystal formation, an ice pop is frozen while at rest and becomes a solid block of ice. Ready to give them a try?
